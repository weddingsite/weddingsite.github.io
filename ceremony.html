The Ceremony & Reception

The ceremony will take place at 5:00 PM at the Sensory Garden in the Minnesota Landscape Arboretum. Guests of our wedding have free admission to the Arboretum all day, so you are encouraged to come early and walk around the beautiful gardens. At the gate, just show your invitation or tell the guard that you are with the Biatek/Marsh wedding.

It looks like we're going to have some crisp fall weather, so make sure to dress warmly. You'll also have to walk down the road a little bit to get to the ceremony site, so comfortable walking shoes are a good idea. The Arboretum also has golf carts that can pick you up and drop you off if that's a concern.

After the ceremony, we will move to the Garden Room, in the Eden Prairie City Center. Be prepared for dinner, dancing, and fun! It's just a short drive from the Arboretum to the Garden Room. Here is a map with directions to help you out.

Hotel information

If you are attending from out of town, there are a number of hotels in the area. We can recommend the Fairfield Inn in Eden Prairie, family members have stayed there before and had a good experience and it's fairly inexpensive. If you are coming from out of town, please email us and tell us where you're staying so that we can leave a welcome package for you!
